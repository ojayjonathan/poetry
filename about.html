The story behind "Developing the Love for Poetry"

Joan Mokeira

Literature Student, Egerton University

Welcome to my poetry project! I am Joan Mokeira, a passionate literature student at Egerton University with a deep love for the written word. This project, "Developing the Love for Poetry," represents my journey through the beautiful and complex world of poetry.

This website serves as both a showcase of poetic works and an educational resource for those who wish to deepen their understanding of poetry. Whether you're a fellow student, a poetry enthusiast, or someone just beginning to explore verse, I hope you find something here that speaks to your heart.
